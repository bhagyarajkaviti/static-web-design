Pakistan vs England: Harry Brook can be a successful all-format player like Virat Kohli, says Ben Stokes

By India Today Web Desk: England Test skipper Ben Stokes reckoned that Harry Brook has the talent to taste success in equal proportion in all three formats, like that of the former Indian skipper Virat Kohli. Stokes put forth his views after Brook became the Player of the Match following England’s 26-run win in the second Test against Babar Azam’s Pakistan at the Multan Cricket Stadium. Brook faltered in the first innings, getting out to debutant Abrar Ahmed, but he made amends in the second, scoring 108 runs with 14 fours and one six.
